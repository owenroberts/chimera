Each position represents part of the arc of your life.

The positions should be read left to right, alternating up and down.

~ Today

~ Your nature

~ Your fears

~ You need

~ Avoid

~ Embrace

~ You want

~ Your hopes

~ Your reality

~ Tomorrow

~ The future

Each chimera is a hybrid of three animals. Each animals carries a meaning.

~ Cat represents secrecy.

~ Bird represents greed.

~ Fish represents creation.

~ Pig represents cunning.

~ Dog represents loyalty.

~ Goat represents evil.

~ Lizard represents change.

~ Mosquito represents perception.

~ Owl represents wisdom.

~ Rabbit represents fertility.

~ Ram represents force.

~ Rat represents timidity.

The body part of each chimera changes the meeting.

Head ~ Your unconscious self.

Body ~ Your natural self.

Rear ~ You percieved self.
